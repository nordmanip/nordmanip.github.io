Building bridges across Nordic robotics

The Nordic Robotic Manipulation Network brings together researchers from universities and institutes across the Nordic countries who share a passion for advancing robotic manipulation.

Our mission is to foster collaboration, share knowledge, and accelerate progress in manipulation research through regular interactions and annual gatherings. Whether you work on grasping, deformable objects, mobile manipulation, or vision-language-action models, there's a place for you here.

Founded in 2026, we aim to create a vibrant community where ideas flow freely across borders and institutions.
